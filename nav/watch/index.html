A final message sent from the eccentric billionaire & mentor, Dr. Wurbly Turbly.

His previously, terminally ill son, Wurbly Jr., has made arrangements after his father's death, seeing an unexpected recovery.

Wurbly Sr. instead entrusted each of his 3 closest pupils with finalizing his will by continuing the research he left behind.

In the process of doing so, they'll form a special pact, by extension, inheriting his property, “Fort Tres” , as a reward to advance their efforts.
They are instructed to reside there, follow his instructions, & form “The Trifect.”
